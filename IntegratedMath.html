Course Description

This course focuses on the development of Algebra 1 skills and applications. Prior to beginning this course, students are expected to have mastered topics including but not limited to operations on integers, fractions and decimals. Topics of this course include but are not limited to linear, quadratic and exponential functions.


Office Hours/Tutoring/Extra Help

At some point during the course of this year, you will struggle and need to get help. This course is designed with a student-centered, student-driven approach. I encourage all of my students to take ownership of their education and to see assistance when stuck or if they don't understand something. The first and most important time for you to take advantage of me is during class time. However, if additional help is required or necessary please contact me in person, or via email toarrange a time to meet with me. In general, before school is the best option to get additional help. I arrive by 7:30 each morning, feel free to simply stop by my room. After school tends to be a little trickier due to family obligations and other work related obligations.
